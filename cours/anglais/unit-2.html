Changing bodies and diet :

Vidéo

The document is an interview for the American TV show “Good Morning America” that was broadcast in 2018. A new study warns parents not to talk about dieting to their kids. In fact, they put a lot of pressure on the teen’s shoulders who are already under a lot of pressure from their peers and the social medias. It is a time in their life when they are not comfortable with the changes in their bodies. So, if their child needs dieting or losing weight; it could be a good idea to seek help from a professional. Because parents are regarded as models, it is important for them to walk the walk.
